BooST.Me Mantra


To provide a unique digital platform that aims to impart high quality education, by combining the best international business standards & practices with new age technologies and methodologies, to bring together proficient tutors and enthusiastic students.



Learner Centric

The BooST.Me platform is centered around how students can make the most of their limited time and resources. Students can use various search parameters to quickly and easily find tutors to match their exact requirements. Students can get on-demand tutoring anywhere, anytime.

To become a BooST.Me-certfied tutor, each tutor has to undergo a registration process to ensure that our students learn from the very best tutors available.
